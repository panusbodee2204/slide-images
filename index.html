Why a Dedicated Unit?

Creating high-quality Video Content requires a fundamentally different workflow from Editorial writing.

To scale our IP and generate new revenue streams, we need a specialized unit that separates Production Logic (Script, Shoot, Post) from Editorial Logic (Daily News).


The Goal:

To build new IPs beyond "News" that are quality-driven and sponsor-friendly.

Key Differences

  • Editorial: Speed, Accuracy, Text-heavy.
  • VDO Unit: Pre-production, Storytelling, Visual Engagement.
